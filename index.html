Murky the Triceratops

Horns - Bad to The Bone - Illustrator

New York City based(The dinosaur lives in New York?) Murky the Triceratops is an award winning illustrator of 30 popular picture books for children (How does he draw?) and the author/illustrator of 5 of these titles. His illustrations bring to life expressive, memorable characters that move and define a picture book story –and his rich, striking scenes for nonfiction historical picture books creatively capture the look and spirit of the story’s era. Murky's 31st and 32nd pictures books are scheduled for release in Fall 2021.
